About

Orbitstation - Hand Crafted Wood Games

I am so happy to be a toy maker. I create and design all of my games from scratch. To accomplish this, I have had to learn Illustrator, LightBurn, and Xtool Software. Over the past year I have been happy to be tooling up my Massachusetts based workshop.


The final transformation, including the packaging, instructions, artwork, design, laser cutting, and actual assembly, are done right here in the USA.


However, parts of the XTool laser are from China, the raw wood is Baltic Birch, and the raw cards are printed in Hong Kong. So, While I can’t say “Made in the USA”, I am proud to be able to say “Designed, Laser Cut and Assembled in the USA.


I am now proud to have my own registered trademark Orbitstation llc. under this trade mark I am creating card games, hand-crafted wooden toys, and fun print & play titles.


I have protected my designs/source files and artwork with Trademarks and all the appropriate Copywrites © and LLC paper work. So please, kindly think twice before trying to copy my stuff.
